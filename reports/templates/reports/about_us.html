About the App

RoadEscape is a mobile app that empowers commuters to collaboratively flag and identify areas of high traffic and roads with obstructions such as constructions, accidents, parades, flooding and etc. The app aims to provide a platform for users to contribute real-time information about road obstructions, allowing others to make informed decisions about their travel routes.
